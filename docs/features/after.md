# Feature: After

Datatype: `string`

Available for nodetype(s): [`word`](wordnodefeatures.md#readme)

Feature description: After

This feature contains the character (space or punctuation) pressent after a word.

after | explanation | Frequency
---  | --- | ---
' ' | normal space | 119271
',' | comma | 9443
'.' | normal dot | 5717
'·' | high dot | 2355
';' | semicollon | 970
'—' | dash | 30
